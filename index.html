Calculation Methodology

Power Law wind correction: V_h = V_10 × (h/10)^α × s where α 為地形粗糙度指數（海岸 0.10、開闊地 0.14、郊區 0.22、都會 0.33）， s 為遮蔽折減係數。

Forced convection coefficient (rough estimate): h_forced ≈ h_natural × √(1 + V_h / 0.5)，僅作數量級評估，不替代 CFD。

Margin recovery (K): ΔT = (55 − T_p95)，搭配風速增益後等效散熱餘裕。

Volume saving heuristic: 假設散熱器熱阻 θ_HS ∝ 1/h × 1/A_fin，鰭片面積與體積近似線性， 因此 ΔV/V ≈ 1 − θ_HS,new / θ_HS,orig。實務上仍需 CFD 驗證。

Calculation Assumptions & Data Sources

氣候資料來源：Open-Meteo Historical Weather API，免費、無需 API key、CC-BY 4.0 授權。預設抓取近 5 年逐時 (hourly) 資料：temperature_2m（地面 2 公尺氣溫）、wind_speed_10m（地面 10 公尺風速）、wind_direction_10m（地面 10 公尺風向）、shortwave_radiation（短波輻射）。底層資料為 ECMWF ERA5 再分析。

風速高度修正（Power Law 指數律）：Vh = V10 × (h / 10)α × sshield。其中 V10 是地面 10 公尺風速，h 是實際安裝高度，α 是地形粗糙度指數，sshield 是周邊遮蔽物造成的折減係數。

沿海 / 開放海面α = 0.10
開闊地形 / 草地α = 0.14
郊區α = 0.22
密集都市α = 0.33

主導風向判定：取「最熱季節」（北半球 6–9 月、南半球 12–3 月）日間 10:00–18:00 的逐時風資料，把 360° 切成 16 個方位（每格 22.5°），計算頻率分佈後取頻率最高者為主風向。之所以只取熱季日間，是因為這段時間才是散熱最緊迫的時段。

鰭片方向建議規則：鰭片走向設定為平行於主導風向，讓氣流可順暢通過鰭片通道並形成強制對流。若主導風向頻率低於 30%（代表風向太分散），系統會降級為「以自然對流為主」並標示警告，不建議依賴風向帶來的散熱增益。

扇區約束：天線扇區指向通常由訊號涵蓋需求決定、不能任意轉動，所以實際上機體面向只能在天線扇區方向 ±45° 的範圍內微調。系統會在這個約束下，找出最接近主風向的合法方向。

使用限制與注意事項：本工具定位為 佈建前的初步環境評估，不是 CFD 模擬的替代品。最終散熱規格仍需以 FloTHERM、Icepak 等 CFD 軟體驗證關鍵 worst-case。Open-Meteo 屬再分析資料，實際微氣候會因附近建物、植被遮蔽、海陸風、都市熱島等效應而與資料有差異；山區、都市峽谷、近海岸等複雜地形建議搭配在地氣象站實測資料交叉驗證。

Climate data source: Open-Meteo Historical Weather API. Free, no API key, CC-BY 4.0 license. Fetches 5 years of hourly data: temperature_2m, wind_speed_10m, wind_direction_10m, shortwave_radiation. Underlying data is ECMWF ERA5 reanalysis.

Wind speed altitude correction (Power Law): Vh = V10 × (h / 10)α × sshield, where V10 is the 10 m reference wind, h is the actual installation height, α is terrain roughness exponent, and sshield is a reduction factor from nearby obstructions.

Coastal / Open Seaα = 0.10
Open terrain / grasslandα = 0.14
Suburbanα = 0.22
Urban denseα = 0.33

Prevailing wind detection: Uses hourly wind data from the hottest season (Northern hemisphere Jun–Sep, Southern hemisphere Dec–Mar), daytime 10:00–18:00 only. Directions are binned into 16 sectors (22.5° each); the highest-frequency bin is selected as the prevailing direction. Restricting to hot-season daytime isolates the window where cooling is most critical.

Fin orientation rule: Fins are aligned parallel to the prevailing wind so airflow passes cleanly through the fin channels (forced convection). If the prevailing direction frequency is below 30% (wind too scattered), the rule degrades to "natural convection dominant" and a warning is shown — forced-convection gains cannot be reliably exploited.

Sector constraint: Antenna sector orientation is dictated by signal coverage and cannot be freely rotated, so the physical unit can only be turned within ±45° of the antenna bearing. The system finds the best allowed orientation within that window.

Caveats: This tool is a pre-deployment site assessment, not a CFD replacement. Final thermal design must still be validated with FloTHERM / Icepak against worst-case scenarios. Open-Meteo is reanalysis data; real microclimates can diverge due to buildings, vegetation, sea-land breeze, urban heat island, etc. For complex terrain (mountains, urban canyons, coastal sites), cross-validate with local weather station data.
